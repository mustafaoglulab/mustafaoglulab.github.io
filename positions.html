We only accept interns during the summer through the PURE program . This program accepts students from around the world during the summer semester. Selected students have the opportunity to live on the Sabanci University campus for free during the internship period (~2 months). Each semester, Mustafaoglu Lab offers several interesting projects for this program, which you can follow on the program's website.

How to apply:You should apply for these projects through the website PURE. However, you can always email Dr. Mustafaoglu about your enthusiasm for our PURE projects that caught your attention.
