Mustafaoglu Lab

We are an interdisiciplinary group consists of molecular biologists, chemists, nano scientists, and engineers from different focuses. Our aim is to understand the brain and its diseases forms so that we can offer novel therapeutic solutions for them.

We are also believe in "beauty of simplicity". We are finding simple solutions to complex diagnostic problems by designing inexpensive, sensitive, and easy to use biosensor systems that can be used in underdeveloped countries.
